NFP & India — The Connection

💱

INR / USD Impact

Strong NFP = Dollar strengthens = Rupee weakens. Every Indian import and dollar-denominated debt gets more expensive overnight.

🏦

FII/FPI Flows

Strong US jobs = Fed may delay cuts = US bonds more attractive = FIIs pull money OUT of India. Nifty reacts immediately.

📈

Dalal Street Reaction

Gap-up or gap-down on Monday after NFP. India opens after US weekend reaction is priced in. Wolf_Stocks gives you the edge.
